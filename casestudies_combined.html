Healthcare

Revenue Cycle Transformation

300-bed multi-specialty hospital network facing critical cash flow challenges.

Revenue Cycle Analytics Automation

The Challenge

Complex billing systems with no validation layers

Fragmented administrative workflows and legacy manual entry systems lacked necessary validation layers for complex multi-specialty billing. High denial rates and 3-week reconciliation delays were symptoms of a broken process.

Key Issues

  • High denial rates across complex billing codes
  • Manual reconciliation causing significant delays
  • No predictive visibility into claim issues

The Solution

End-to-end revenue cycle redesign

We deployed billing automation with a real-time predictive analytics layer. The result: automated validation at entry point, 24-hour claim-to-cash cycles, and denial rates cut by nearly half.

Our Approach

  • Automated validation at point of entry
  • Real-time predictive denial scoring
  • 24-hour claim-to-cash cycle achieved

Outcome

28% Net Collections Up
42% Denial Rate Down
6 Months Timeline
3.2x ROI Achieved
D2C Strategy

Omnichannel Scaling

Premium skincare brand struggling with high CAC and marketplace dependency.

Brand Positioning E-Commerce Analytics

The Challenge

High CAC with marketplace dependency

High Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and heavy dependency on third-party marketplaces were eroding margins and preventing direct customer ownership. The brand had zero differentiation in a crowded market.

Key Issues

  • 80% revenue from marketplace commissions
  • No first-party customer data
  • Zero brand differentiation

The Solution

Clinical authority + omnichannel strategy

We implemented a brand repositioning focused on clinical authority and a balanced omnichannel strategy to diversify traffic sources. Direct channels now generate 48% of revenue with 40% lower CAC.

Our Approach

  • Clinical authority positioning built
  • D2C channel with 40% lower CAC
  • First-party data platform deployed

Outcome

3.2x Revenue Growth
35% Margin Improvement
62% CAC Reduction
48% Direct Sales Up
